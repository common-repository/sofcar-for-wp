== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I create my Sofcar account?  =

A registry will provide you with an access to our booking management platform.
You will receive confirmation of the creation of the client along with the subscription data and access to the platform in the email address provided. 
From your Sofcar backend, you will be able to define the data of your fleet to setup the booking engine. 

= In how many sites can Sofcar Plugin be installed? =

You can install Sofcar in all your sites and those developed for your customers.
You will access its associated content through your user secret key.

= Is it compatible with WordPress multisite installations? =

Yes, it is fully compatible

= Can I use more than one shortcode in the same page? = 

Yes! You can insert as many shortcodes as you want in your pages. 

= If there are modifications in my fleet, will WP ask for a new synchronisation? = 

Yes! Sofcar plugin will detect and display any changes in your fleet and will also display a request for new synchronisation. 

= Can I use my current WordPress theme?  =

Sure, once installed, Sofcar works with any WordPress theme. Nevertheless, we provide you with a Sofcar Theme already designed to meet your needs.

== Installation ==

You can log in to your WordPress dashboard with an administrator user name and password.

1. Click on the Plugins icon in the menu on the left.
2. Click on the "Add new" button at the top of the Plugins menu.
3. Search for "Sofcar" or Select the plugins option add new. Click on add new and export the previously downloaded add-on.
4. Click on "Install."
5. When the message "Plugin successfully installed" appears, click on the "Activate Plugin" link.
6. If these steps are carried out correctly, you will find a new Sofcar section in your left sidebar. 

= Manual Installation = 

You can also download our Sofcar plugin and upload it to your webserver via your favorite FTP application, to do so, log in to your WordPress dashboard with an administrator user name and password.

1. Download the Sofcar add-on for WordPress. 
2. Upload the unzipped plugin folder to your WordPress plugin folder (wp-content / plugins). 
3. Go to WordPress and click on the Plugins icon in your left sidebar menu. 
4. Activate the add-on. 
5. If these steps are carried out correctly, you will find a new Sofcar section in your left sidebar.

More information: [Sofcar](http://www.sofcar.com)
